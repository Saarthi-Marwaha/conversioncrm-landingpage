What is ConversionCRM?

ConversionCRM is free trial conversion software for SaaS founders and product teams. It combines three things that usually live in separate tools: user activation tracking (one script tag, auto-tracked behavior), customer activation scoring (a 0–100 engagement score across six layers with automatic lifecycle stages), and automated trial conversion emails (eight behavior-triggered emails from welcome to win-back, sent under your name). Teams use it as a lightweight product adoption platform and a trial-to-paid funnel tool — it installs in about 3 minutes and is free during beta.

ConversionCRM ships the whole signup-to-paid engine prebuilt: 6-layer engagement scoring, automatic lifecycle stages, and 8 behavior-triggered emails that react to what users actually do.
